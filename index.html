Start
Level : 0





1.You'll see four colored buttons (e.g., red, green, blue, yellow) on your screen. Each button corresponds to a color and emits a unique sound when pressed.

2.To begin the game, press the "Start" button.

3.Observe the Sequence: Pay close attention as the game presents a sequence of colors and sounds.

4.Replicate the Sequence: After the game presents the sequence, it's your turn to replicate it. Click on the buttons in the same order as they were shown by the game.
